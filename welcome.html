Welcome

This website contains information about my research into cigarette advertisements, especially research into medical journals. Cigarettes advertisements are highly regulated today. Due to health and ethical concerns, there has been a big push to change things. For this reason, cigarette advertisements can tall us a lot about the psychology of advertising. When a market is fresh and information is not publically known, what is hid and what tactics are used to gain customers?

An independent investigation was conducted on Facebook policies regarding cigarette ads. While Facebook did try to prevent dishonest advertising, it was not successful. Ads for hookah tobaccos, e-cigarettes, smokeless tobacco, and cigars were found. All of these types had some ads which lacked age-gating, including 90% of leading cigarette brands.

Site Accessibility

The navigation bar is done using a nav element rather than a generic div element. This allows assistive technologies to identify it as a landmark region.

Images in the gallery have alt text for screen readers. To check this in the typical browser, it is also the hover text (appears when the cursor rests on the image).
