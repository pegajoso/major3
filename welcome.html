Welcome to the Community

This website contains information about research into cigarette advertisements, especially research into medical journals.

An independent investigation was conducted on Facebook policies regarding cigarette ads. While Facebook did try to prevent dishonest advertising, it was not successful. Ads for hookah tobaccos, e-cigarettes, smokeless cigarettes, and cigars were found. All of these types had some ads which lacked age-gating, including 90% of leading cigarette brands.
