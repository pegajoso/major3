Glossary

Philip Morris — An American tobacco company which made many advertisements with celebrities.

tobacco — A plant of the genus Nicotiana. Tobacco plants contain nicotine.

nicotine — The addictive chemical in tobacco.

e-cigarette — An electronic device which allows the user to breathe in nicotine as a vapor.

smokeless tobacco — Electronic devices which contain tobacco plant, unlike e-cigarettes.

age gate — Restricting digital content to a specific age range. Must be 18+ for cigarette ads.
